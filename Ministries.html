Ministries

Men's Ministry

The men's ministry at Mwihoko Baptist is a fellowship of men who are dedicated to serve God. In the fellowship they encourage each other to develop a strong relationship with Christ by knowing whom God is,
they urge themselves to experience God in their lives by transforming themselves in what God wants them to be.
They also make the commmunity know God through conducting outreach programs.

Women's Ministry

As women, we believe in the community and the spirit of walking together as one family in serving the Lord through evangelism, fellowship with other Baptist women.
They are dedicated empower and encourage other women to grow in faith.

Youth Ministry

The Mwihoko Baptist Youth Ministry is headed by Pastor Kennedy Kimani with assistance of other youth leaders.
The ministry is a safe and vibrant space where youths have fun, promote togetherness and fellowship through discipleship.
The main objective of the ministry is to encourage youths to deepen their relationship with God.
In the ministry we also involve ourselves in fun activities that nurture us spiritually and our personal growth.

Children Ministry

At Mwihoko we are thrilled to have children and therefore we assist them walk through faith and understand God's love.
we create a conducive environment where they learn about God and help them develop personal relationship with Jesus and encounter Him in their lives.
By ensuring they discover the Bible truth, we offer them interactive and fun classes through Bible storytelling, praise songs and hand-on activities.
