Our Beliefs & Practices

1. Authority of God's Word

As a Baptist church, Bible is our ultimate authority for faith and practice.

2. The Holy Trinity

As Baptists we believe in one God who exits in three persons: Father, Son(Jesus Christ), and Holy Spirit.

3. Salvation by Faith

We believe that salvation is through God's grace received by faith in Jesus Christ.

4. Baptism

We conduct believers baptism which is done to an individual who have made a concious decision to follow Jesus Christ through immersion in water.

5. Church Government

As a Baptist church we are independent(separated from the state) and has the authority to govern ourselves lead by the pastor as the minister.

6. Denomination Information

Mwihoko Baptist Church is a religious organization under Nairobi North Baptist Association headed by the Baptist Convention of Kenya
